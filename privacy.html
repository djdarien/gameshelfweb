Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

GameShelf is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal information to provide the best gaming organization experience.

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we store your email address and basic profile information.
  • Game Library Data: We store information about the games you add to your collection, including titles, progress, and platforms.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymized usage data to improve the app's performance and features.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve GameShelf's services:

  • To sync your collection across your iOS devices via iCloud.
  • To provide personalized game recommendations based on your library.
  • To provide customer support when requested.

3. Data Storage & Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Most of your game data is stored directly in your personal iCloud container, which is encrypted by Apple. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@gameshelf.app.
