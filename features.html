Steam Library Sync

Easily import your entire Steam collection with a single ID. We respect your privacy: no login required, just enter your manual ID to populate your shelf in seconds.

Your data belongs to you. Export your entire collection as a CSV for backup, or import from other tools easily.

Edit your shelf efficiently. Use bulk delete or quick status changes via long-press for a fast and fluid experience.

Designed natively for iOS with full haptic feedback and beautiful empty states. Experience a polished dark theme that looks incredible on OLED displays.
